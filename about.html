About

Author, Editor, and Educator currently based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Laura Nejako graduated from the Pennsylvania State University with a master’s degree in English. While there, she completed a collection of fictional short stories titled Why We Don’t Talk. This collection examines the unspoken conflict that occurs in human interaction and how this silence is often subtle and dramatic in being unannounced – prompting meditation on never truly being able to understand another person. In the years that followed, she decided to revisit this topic through a new lens – humor writing.

Laura is currently working on her upcoming book, Let’s Become Strangers, as well as an epistolary project with her grandmother and great aunt.

If you would like to connect with Laura, she is available for a range of writing, editing, and speaking opportunities.
